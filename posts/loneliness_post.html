![city_loneliness.jpg](/images/city_loneliness.jpg) # loneliness The strange feeling is not letting you be alone When I was a kid I asked myself if it was wrong to be alone and do whatever I wanted with my life. After some time being alone, a strange feeling came to me with the name of loneliness, I found life meaning less and without any color. it is really painful, everything became black and white, pain and sorrow. Then I realized everyone needs a good friend, a good family, and true love to be truly alive :)
